// Capabilities

The fundamentals I design, build and operate for you.

Compute & Virtualization

Proxmox/KVM and VMware optional. Clean host layouts, backups, lifecycle and HA where needed.

  • Resource planning and sizing
  • Storage with ZFS/Ceph/NFS
  • Snapshots, backup and DR
Containers & Orchestration

Docker/Compose for small setups, K3s/Kubernetes for clusters. Secure defaults and GitOps.

  • Ingress (Traefik/Caddy/Nginx)
  • Secrets and registry management
  • Service mesh and policies (optional)
Networking

Solid foundations: routing, VLANs, firewalls and secure remote access.

  • Firewalls and segmentation
  • WireGuard/OpenVPN site-to-site
  • DNS, certificates and zero-downtime cutovers
Data & Observability

Dependable data services and actionable metrics/logs from day one.

  • PostgreSQL/MariaDB/Redis
  • Prometheus, Grafana, Alertmanager
  • Loki/ELK logging with retention controls
